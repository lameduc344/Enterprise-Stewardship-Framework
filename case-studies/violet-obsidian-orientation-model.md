# Violet Obsidian Orientation Model

**Status:** Working case-study doctrine  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Date:** 2026-06-19

## Portfolio Classification

Violet Obsidian is presently treated as a **Mission Asset** with long-horizon institutional potential.

Its first complete member transformation should not be measured by the number of features, chambers, or pages available. It should be measured by whether a new member can enter uncertain and leave oriented, understood, and placed into a meaningful course of study.

## First Complete Transformation

A member enters asking:

> Who am I, what patterns am I carrying, and where should I begin?

The member leaves with:

- a strong natal chart reading;
- a structured Violet Obsidian reflection;
- a current developmental profile;
- a recommended study path;
- a first lesson or assignment;
- a visible record of why that path was selected.

## Core Orientation Flow

### 1. Birth Data and Context Intake

The member provides:

- birth date;
- birth time, including confidence level where exact time is uncertain;
- birth location;
- present goals, questions, and concerns;
- self-reported areas of strength, tension, or confusion.

Because this information is sensitive, the system should clearly explain why it is collected, how it is stored, who can access it, and how the member may request deletion.

### 2. Natal Chart Reading

The natal chart reading functions as an orientation instrument rather than a deterministic judgment.

The reading may explore:

- identity and self-expression;
- emotional tendencies;
- learning and communication patterns;
- discipline and resistance;
- leadership and service;
- relationship to community;
- vocation and material development;
- recurring developmental tensions.

The chart proposes patterns for consideration. It does not declare permanent limits or replace medical, psychological, legal, financial, or other professional diagnosis.

### 3. Violet Obsidian Reflection

The member responds to the chart and supplies lived evidence.

Suggested prompts include:

- What felt accurate?
- What felt incomplete or incorrect?
- Which patterns have appeared repeatedly in your life?
- Where are you strongest?
- Where do you avoid responsibility?
- What knowledge are you missing?
- What are you trying to build?
- What obligations do you carry to yourself, family, and community?

The member's reflection should carry more weight than automated interpretation when determining the recommended path.

### 4. Developmental Profile

Violet Obsidian combines the chart, reflection, and stated goals into a current developmental profile.

Examples:

- strong vision, weak structure;
- strong communication, weak confidence;
- high service orientation, limited financial discipline;
- strong civic interest, limited foundational knowledge;
- entrepreneurial drive, scattered execution;
- spiritual curiosity, weak historical grounding.

This profile is provisional and should change as the member studies, reflects, and acts.

### 5. Recommended Study Path

Possible entry paths include:

- Identity and Self-Knowledge
- Moorish History and Civics
- Law, Rights, and Responsibilities
- Governance and Community
- Enterprise and Economic Stewardship
- AI Literacy and Technological Power
- Communication and Cultural Production
- Discipline, Health, and Personal Order

Each recommendation should explain why the selected path is likely to strengthen the member's present development.

### 6. First Act of Study

The orientation should immediately produce:

- one lesson;
- one reflection prompt;
- one practical action;
- one short assessment;
- one follow-up or return point.

The experience should end in movement, not only interpretation.

## Institutional Meaning

This orientation model makes Violet Obsidian more than an astrology product.

It performs four institutional functions:

1. receives the person;
2. interprets their present condition;
3. places them into a course of development;
4. records the reasoning and progress.

## Guardrail

> The chart proposes patterns. The member supplies lived evidence. Violet Obsidian recommends a path.

## Enterprise Health Implication

For Violet Obsidian, early health should be measured by whether this orientation flow is complete, trusted, repeatable, and capable of guiding members into meaningful study—not by the total number of available platform features.
